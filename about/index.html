Why Bidly?

From everyday gems to rare finds, our listings are curated to surprise and delight. Placing a bid is just a click away. Stay in control and up-to-date with real-time bidding. Every listing has an expiry, making the experience exciting and competitive. Bidly is powered by Noroffs Auction House API, ensuring secure and trustworthy bidding for all users.

The Mission

Bidly was created as a semester project at Noroff School of Technology and Digital Media. The goal was to provide a functional, responsive, and engaging auction experience, while showcasing the power of frontend development with HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript.
